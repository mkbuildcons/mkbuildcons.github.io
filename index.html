About Us

MK Advisors & Buildcons is an experienced, rapidly expanding company of dedicated workforce with varied and extensive building construction management experience. Innovation has always been a central part of MK Advisors & Buildcons approach in the field of industrial structures, offices, residences. The firm provides complete solution starting from architect, civil, electrical, plumbing, interiors work.

M/S MK Advisors & Buildcons is based in Gurgaon under the leadership of Er. Manoj Kumar. Manoj Kumar is a graduate civil engineer and having more than 24 years of experience in the various aspects on building construction.
